# Module 6: Bias & Limitation Detector

## Purpose
This module identifies potential biases and limitations in the research paper to help readers critically evaluate the findings.

## Functionality
- Analyze methodology, sample size, and data sources for possible bias.
- Detect limitations explicitly stated by the authors.
- Suggest standardized warnings when limitations are unclear or missing.
