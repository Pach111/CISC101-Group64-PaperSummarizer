# Module 5: Citation Extractor

## Purpose
Extract citations and references from the research paper.

## Responsibilities
- Identify citation markers (e.g., [1], (Smith, 2020)).
- Collect references from bibliography or reference section.
- Present citations in a structured list.

## Constraints
- Do not invent citations.
- Only extract what is present in the paper.

## Output
- Citation list in Markdown format.
