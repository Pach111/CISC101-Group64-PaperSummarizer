# Module 6: Equation Explainer

## Purpose
Detect and explain key equations in the paper.

## Responsibilities
- Identify mathematical equations in text.
- Provide concise explanations:
  - What the equation represents.
  - Its role in the paper.
  - Simplified meaning for layperson audience.
- Ensure explanations are accurate and evidence-based.

## Constraints
- Do not fabricate equations.
- Explanations must remain concise and clear.

## Output
- Equation list with explanations in Markdown format.
