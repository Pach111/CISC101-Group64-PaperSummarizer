# Module 4: Rendering & Refinement

## Purpose
Format and refine the final output for clarity and audience needs.

## Responsibilities
- Assemble final structured output in Markdown.
- Ensure consistent formatting across sections.
- Generate two summary variants:
  - Expert summary (dense, technical).
  - Layperson summary (simplified, jargon-free).
- Include:
  - Overall paper summary.
  - Section-by-section summary table.
  - Mini-glossary of key terms.
  - Checks & warnings.

## Constraints
- Maintain clarity and readability.
- Respect audience differences (expert vs layperson).

## Output
- Complete Markdown-formatted summary package.
