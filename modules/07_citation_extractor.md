# Module 7: Citation Extractor

## Purpose
This module extracts citations and references directly from the research paper to ensure that all sources are properly acknowledged.

## Functionality
- Scan the paper for reference sections, in-text citations, and footnotes.
- Normalize citation formats (APA/MLA/Chicago).
- Output a standardized list of references.

## Rules
- Do not invent or fabricate citations.
- If a citation is incomplete, flag it with a standardized warning.
- Maintain strict evidence mode: only use information present in the paper.

## Output
- A Markdown-formatted list of citations.
- Warnings for missing or incomplete references.
